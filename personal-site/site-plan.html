Overview

Purpose

To promote and sell delicious, authentic South African food from a local restaurant that provides a vibrant, welcoming space for families, youth, and tourists. The site will showcase the menu, drinks, events, and create an online presence for orders, bookings, and community engagement.

Audience

Families looking for a fun place to eat together. Young adults and students seeking cool hangout spots. Tourists who want to taste real South African cuisine and culture. Locals who want a lively, relaxing place with music, food, and drinks.

Dynamic elements

This website includes a slideshow presenting featured dishes of the restaurant.
When the "Place an order" is clicked, a registration form shows up, allowing them to submit an order.
The feedback form asks customers for their feedback and send a thank-you message when they submit the form.
On the Menu page, customers can add their favorite dishes to their cart, and an alert message appears confirming that the dish has been successfully added to the cart.

Home page

1. Welcome to Chichi's
At Chichi's, we’re all about authentic South African flavour, good times, and unforgettable moments. From mouthwatering shisa nyama to refreshing cocktails and a laid-back atmosphere, this is the ultimate hangout spot for families, friends, foodies, and fun-seekers. Whether you're stopping by for a chilled drink, a weekend braai, or just good conversation under the sun, you'll feel right at home. Come for the food. Stay for the vibes.
